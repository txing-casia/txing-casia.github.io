Motion Transformer with Global Intention Localization and Local Movement Refinement

Abstract

  • 通过使用稠密的候选目标方式（density of goal candidates）可以使得预测变得高效。本文提出了Motion TRansformer (MTR)框架，将运动预测变为“全局意图定位的联合优化”和“局部运动精调”两个任务。

  • 本文没有使用目标候选，而是使用一小组可学习的运动查询对（motion query pairs）来融合空间意图先验。每个运动查询对负责特定运动模式的轨迹预测和细化，这稳定了训练过程并促进了更好的多模态预测。

  • 代码开源：https://github.com/sshaoshuai/MTR

1 Introduction

  • 现有预测方法主要分为两类：

    • the goal-based methods：采用稠密的目标候选（goal candidates）覆盖所有可能的agent运动终点，预测器预测哪个终点是最有可能的，并且补全到终点的完整轨迹。较少的候选终点会降低性能，而较多的候选终点会大大增加计算和存储成本；
      • Junru Gu, Chen Sun, and Hang Zhao. Densetnt: End-to-end trajectory prediction from dense goal sets. In ICCV, 2021.
      • Hang Zhao, Jiyang Gao, Tian Lan, Chen Sun, Benjamin Sapp, Balakrishnan Varadarajan, Yue Shen, Yi Shen, Yuning Chai, Cordelia Schmid, et al. Tnt: Target-driven trajectory prediction. In CoRL, 2020.
    • the direct-regression methods：通过encode的agent特征直接预测一个轨迹集合。这种方法的预测轨迹灵活性比较好，但通常收敛较慢（相同的输入特征却会有多模态的行为，回归收敛较慢）；模型会倾向于预测数据中最常见的模态；
      • Jiquan Ngiam, Vijay Vasudevan, Benjamin Caine, Zhengdong Zhang, Hao-Tien Lewis Chiang, Jeffrey Ling, Rebecca Roelofs, Alex Bewley, Chenxi Liu, Ashish Venugopal, et al. Scene transformer: A unified architecture for predicting future trajectories of multiple agents. In ICLR, 2022.
      • Balakrishnan Varadarajan, Ahmed Hefny, Avikalp Srivastava, Khaled S Refaat, Nigamaa Nayakanti, Andre Cornman, Kan Chen, Bertrand Douillard, Chi Pang Lam, Dragomir Anguelov, et al. Multipath++: Efficient information fusion and trajectory aggregation for behavior prediction. In ICRA, 2022.

  • 本文提出的MTR（Motion TRansformer）结合了上述两个方法的优点：没有依靠稠密的候选终点；有灵活自适应地局部精调；

  • MTR需要完成两个任务：

    • 全局意图定位任务（global intention localization task）：粗略地分类智能体的意图；（实现更高的效率）
    • 局部运动精调任务（local movement refinement task）：自适应地精调每种意图的预测轨迹；（实现更高的精度）

  • 每个运动查询对包括两个部分：

    • 一个静态的意图query；用于全局意图定位
    • 一个动态的搜索query；用于局部运动精调

  • 辅助任务：已有的工作主要利用过去的交互关系忽略了未来的交互信息，未来补偿这些信息，本文使用了一个简单的辅助回归head去密集地预测agent未来的轨迹和速度，并将其编码为额外的未来信息特征用于预测感兴趣agent 的未来轨迹；

  • 主要贡献点：

    • 提出了 motion query pair，处理两个任务；
    • 提出了 auxiliary dense future prediction task，预测符合场景交互的轨迹；
    • 提出了 MTR 框架，将transformer encoder-decoder 架构用于多模态运动预测

  • Our approach achieves state-of-the-art performance on both the marginal and joint motion prediction benchmarks of Waymo Open Motion Dataset (WOMD), outperforming previous best ensemble-free approaches with +8.48% mAP gains for marginal motion prediction and +7.98% mAP gains for joint motion prediction.

  • MTR变体：Shaoshuai Shi, Li Jiang, Dengxin Dai, and Bernt Schiele. Mtr-a: 1st place solution for 2022 waymo open dataset challenge–motion prediction. arXiv preprint arXiv:2209.10033, 2022.

3.3 Training Losses

  • 两个loss：
    • L1 regression loss，优化
    • Gaussian regression loss，使用 negative log-likelihood loss，最大化 the likelihood of ground-truth trajectory
      • 使用hard-assignment strategy，选择离真值轨迹最近的query pair作为正的高斯分量进行优化，这个选择是根据意图point 到 GT trajectory终点的距离计算的；
      • 高斯回归损失在每个decoder layer使用，高斯loss最终的loss和使用相等的权重；

Implementation details

  • For the context encoding, we stack 6 transformer encoder layers.

  • The road map is represented as multiple polylines, where each polyline contains up to 20 points (about 10m in WOMD).

  • We select nearest map polylines around the interested agent. The number of neighbors in encoder’s local self-attention is set to 16. The encoder hidden feature dimension is set as D = 256. For the decoder modules, we stack 6 decoder layers. L is set to 128 to collect the closest map polylines from context encoder for motion refinement.

  • By default, we utilize 64 motion query pairs where their intention points are generated by conducting k-means clustering algorithm on the training set. To generate 6 future trajectories for evaluation, we use non-maximum suppression (NMS) to select top 6 predictions from 64 predicted trajectories by calculating the distances between their endpoints, and the distance threshold is set as 2.5m. Please refer to Appendix for more details.

Training details

  • Our model is trained in an end-to-end manner by AdamW optimizer with a learning rate of 0.0001 and batch size of 80 scenes.

  • We train the model for 30 epochs with 8 GPUs (NVDIA RTX 8000), and the learning rate is decayed by a factor of 0.5 every 2 epochs from epoch 20. The weight decay is set as 0.01 and we do not use any data augmentation.

MTR-e2e for end-to-end motion prediction

We also propose an end-to-end variant of MTR, called MTR-e2e, where only 6 motion query pairs are adopted so as to remove NMS post processing. In the training process, instead of using static intention points for target assignment as in MTR, MTR-e2e selects positive mixture component by calculating the distances between its 6 predicted trajectories and the GT trajectory, since 6 intention points are too sparse to well cover all potential future motions.

  • e2e版本由于query pair比较少，在分配轨迹时候使用了另一种策略——使用预测轨迹和GT轨迹的距离选择轨迹，而不是像MTR中根据intention point点位置进行选择；

Table 1 also shows the comparison of MTR variants. MTR-e2e achieves better minADE and minFDE by removing NMS post-processing, while MTR achieves better mAP since it learns explicit meaning of each motion query pair that produces more confident intention predictions. We also propose a simple model ensemble strategy to merge the predictions of MTR and MTR-e2e and utilize NMS to remove redundant predictions (denoted as MTR-ens), and it takes the best of both models and achieves much better mAP. By adopting such ensemble strategy to 7 variants of our framework (e.g., more decoder layers, different number of queries, larger hidden dimension), our advanced ensemble results (denoted as MTR-Advanced-ens) achieve best performance on the test set leaderboard.

5 Conclusion

  • 提出的基于transformer的双层联合优化结构比较新颖，取得了略优于multipath++的结果，在2022.05取得了waymo预测比赛的1st
  • 局限:
    • 采用了agent-centric strategy预测多模态轨迹，导致相同场景有多个agent的时候会将冗余的场景信息进行编码；
    • rule-based post-processing会导致次优的minADE, minFDE；
    • 如何从多个模态的预测中选取指定的轨迹也是未来可以优化的方向；
